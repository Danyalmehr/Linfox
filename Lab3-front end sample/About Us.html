About Us

Who Are We

Beachvolleyball.com.au is dedicated to expanding the love for beach volleyball around the Sydney area. Centrally based at the iconic Manly and Maroubra beaches, we are an open community welcoming everybody from local to international.
We have a dedicated staff of coaches and referees ready to help you learn the sport, improve your skills, help you compete, and have fun!
A number of our passionate coaches are elite competitive players from Australia and around the globe.
Their enthusiasm for beach volleyball extends from the world stage all the way to their coaching philosophy at every level.
Don't miss out on the opportunity to learn from some of the best Australia and our internationals coaches have to offer!

History

After 3 time Olympian Kerry Pottharst won Gold at the Sydney 2000 Olympic Games, she joined forces with Victor Anfiloff, the now current head of Volleyball Australia's beach volleyball program.
They founded Sydney Beach Volleyball School academy to create a beach volley hub and to make it more people accessible for people of all levels to engage with the sport.
Beachvolleyball.com.au has improved and grown to be an integral part of the volleyball community in Sydney and Australia with the help of many passionate people over the years.

It has been a fantastic start to the 2018 FIVB World Tour with Australia to have hosted two World Tour events. Australian citizen athletes now have the opportunity to be considered for the upcoming International World Tour Events held across several countries in June.
