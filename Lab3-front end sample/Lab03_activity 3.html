Who can play?

There are 800 million players worldwide who play volleyball at least once a week
Volleyball does not require extreme fitness. Thus, it’s great for people who want a low-impact sport that will help them get fit without pushing them to their limit
For those who are a bit more competitive, the sport is fantastic because games can be really exciting and intense
Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, and men and women alike

Junior Beach Volleyball

We classify juniors to be anyone under the age of 19 and we have plenty of Beach Volleyball to share with our juniors.
Entry level Beach Volleyball starts with SpikeZone, our Mini Volley modified version of volleyball which caters for children aged 8-13 years.
The South Australian Beach Volleyball Series (SABVS) also offers a great competition for our Junior Beachie's on nearly every second Saturday's throughout the summer.
With the option to just play one, two, three or all rounds, the SABVS is a competitive competition, yet commitment free - you don't need to commit to the whole season just play the events that suits you best. There are plenty of opportunities to play beach volleyball in South Australia, regardless of your age! Get involved in out Twilight Social Weekly Competitions or our Sunset Volley Sessions for some fun with your mates or if you want to take it more seriously, enter into a South Australian Beach Volleyball Series (SABVS) event throughout the summer and compete in a competitive pairs competition while earning SABVS ranking points to work your way up the ladder. If you're not sure where you fit in, try out one of our training sessions with our friendly coaches or try one-off social events.
